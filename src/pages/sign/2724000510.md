ID-55 east at I-84.  Take I-84 west to Caldwell and Ontario.  I-84 east towards Boise.  ID-55 joins I-84 east to Meridian.  This is a new interchange, completed in 2007.  Before this, all ID-55 traffic was routed through downtown Nampa to get to the interstate.
